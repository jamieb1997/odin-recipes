Croissant French Toast

A delicious spin on the classic french toast

You've heard of French toast, how about you try it with a croissant instead? Go on, give it a try!

Ingredients

  • 4 large croissants, halved horizontally, left out overnight
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 95 degrees C
  2. whisk some eggs, half and half, sugar, vanilla, cinnamon, salt, and nutmeg into a bowl. Dip the croissants into the mixture, flipping and pressing down until each one is coated.
  3. Melt the butter into a large skillet that is on medium heat. Add the croissants cut in half. Fry them until the halves are brown on both sides while turning them periodically.
  4. Enjoy your meal!
