Tomato Soup

A favourite classic

Tomato soup is a popular choice for many people. It's a simple dish to make, but it is very rich in flavour. It is perfect to dip a slice of bread, serve as a starter or a main meal.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups chopped fresh tomatoes
  • 1 slice onion
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons white sugar, or to taste

Steps

  1. Gather all of the ingredients that you need to create the soup.
  2. Get a stockpot and put it on a medium heat.
  3. Add tomatoes, onions, garlic cloves, and chicken broth.
  4. Bring the pot to a boil, then let it boil slowly over 20 minutes.
  5. Turn off the heat and pour the mixture through a food mill and into a bowl.
  6. Melt butter over a medium heat using the stockpot. Add flower to make a roux, and cook it until it's a medium brown.
  7. Slowly pour in the tomato mixture in to the stockpot while whisking.
  8. Add sugar and salt
  9. Enjoy your meal!
