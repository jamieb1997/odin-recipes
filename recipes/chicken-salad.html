Chicken Salad

A quick and easy meal to make

This is a classic dish that everyone is familiar with. All you need are six simple ingredients and 10 minutes of your spare time. Once you have created it, you can put it on bread, salad or wraps.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup blanched slivered almonds
  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 cups chopped, cooked chicken meat
  • 1 stalk celery, chopped

Steps

  1. Have all of the ingredients that you need.
  2. Put almonds into a frying pan and toast them over a medium heat. Shake the almonds frequently while toasting them. Please keep an eye on them, almonds can burn very easily.
  3. Mix the lemon juice, pepper and mayonnaise into a bowl.
  4. Throw in the chicken, almonds and celery.
  5. That's it! Enjoy your meal!
