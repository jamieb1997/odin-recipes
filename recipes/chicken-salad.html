Chicken Salad

A quick and easy meal to make

This is a classic dish that everyone is familiar with. All you need are six simple ingredients and 10 minutes of your spare time. Once you have created it, you can put it on bread, salad or wraps.

Ingredients

    • ½ cup blanched slivered almonds
      ½ cup mayonnaise
      1 tablespoon lemon juice
      ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
      2 cups chopped, cooked chicken meat
      1 stalk celery, chopped
